The Opportunity

Why Japan?

Japan is entering a transformative moment in its innovation landscape, driven by strong government support for startups, universities accelerating research commercialisation, and major corporations actively seeking external innovation partners. At the same time, regional cities are emerging as vibrant innovation hubs, attracting global attention and investment.

Despite Japan's world‑class research capabilities, the number of research‑based startups remains relatively low — creating a unique opportunity for organisations that can bridge academia, industry, and entrepreneurship.

Why Voera?

We don't advise from a distance. We are embedded inside Japan's ecosystem — with the networks, the local presence, and the operator mindset to move things forward on your behalf.

01
Japan-Based & Multi‑City Presence

Based in Kanazawa with active connections across Tokyo and Osaka, we bridges regional ecosystems with global markets. We understand Japan from the inside and help international partners navigate it with confidence.

02
Global Team With Deep Institutional & Expert Networks

Our connection spans multiple countries and continents, giving us a truly global perspective. Combined with strong relationships across universities and research institutions, we provide access to talent, insights, and expertise that support both academic and commercial innovation.

03
Flexible, Friendly, Human‑Centered Support

We’re not a rigid consulting firm, we’re approachable, adaptable, and ready to help in whatever way you need. Whether it’s casual guidance, structured consulting, or hands‑on collaboration, we bring a supportive, people‑first approach to every engagement.

04
Two-Way Bridge

We help overseas startups enter Japan and Japanese companies expand globally, supporting market entry, DX transformation, and cross‑border collaboration. Our mission is to make global movement seamless in both directions.
